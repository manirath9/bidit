## The thesis

The standard service marketplace (Thumbtack, Houzz, etc.) makes the **customer** chase **vendors**. The customer fills a form, gets bombarded by 5+ pushy sales calls, and ends up overpaying because vendors don't compete on price.

**Bidit reverses the loop.**

1. The customer posts what they need (one form, one time).
2. Pre-vetted local vendors **bid down** to win the job.
3. The customer sees only the final offers — sorted by price, rating, and verified credentials.
4. They pick. The vendor gets paid through Bidit's escrow.

The vendor competition does the price discovery. The customer never gets cold-called. The marketplace takes a fixed cut, never inflates with hidden fees.

## How it works for...

### Customers
1. Open https://justbidit.ai → describe what you need (e.g., "house cleaning every 2 weeks for a 3-bedroom in Pointe-Calumet")

## Trust mechanics

- **Vendor identity verified** — government ID + insurance proof + credential (license #, professional accreditation)
- **Reviews are verified-purchase only** — no anonymous Yelp-style brigading. Only customers who actually paid through Bidit can leave a review.
- **Escrow on every job** — money held until customer confirms job complete. Vendor paid out T+2.
- **Dispute resolution** — Bidit arbitrates. Both parties submit evidence. Decisions are public (with PII redacted) so the system is auditable.
- **Quebec Loi 25 / Canadian PIPEDA compliant** — customer data lives in Canadian regions only. No tracking pixels. No paid ads using customer data.

## Manifesto

We believe the standard service marketplace is broken because it monetizes friction:

- **It makes the customer the prey.** "Get 5 quotes!" = 5 cold calls you didn't want.
- **It makes pricing opaque.** Hidden referral fees. Surge pricing. Premium placements that buy preferential treatment.
- **It launders trust.** Anyone with a credit card and a smile can list.

Bidit's design choices reverse all three:

| Old marketplace | Bidit |
|---|---|
| Customer fills a form, gets called by 5 pushy vendors | Customer posts once, vendors bid silently |
| Vendor pays for premium placement | Vendor competes on price + verified credentials |
| Anonymous reviews | Verified-purchase reviews only |
| Hidden fees | Flat published commission, escrowed |
| "Boost your post for $$" | No boost. No paid promotion. Ever. |
| Surge pricing | Fixed bidding window, no algorithmic manipulation |
